assortedbeads updated:

  • Recipe for crossbow bolts moved to guns section, right under powered crossbow.
  • Crossbow recipe edited slightly to include glass, representing a poor scope.
  • Crossbow has a minor zoom factor now, 3 spaces when used.
  • Bolts now embed on hit and can be extracted from kills in bolt form to be used again. Prior to this they turned into metal rods on hits.
  • Embedded bolts are sharp, dealing pain, brute and toxin like normal shrapnel if you run without removing them. They can be removed via surgery or right clicking to yank.
  • Recipe for bolts costs the same, but you now spend 5 metal rods to make a handful of 5 bolts rather than 5 individual bolts, less clicks on players side.
  • You now get chat messages telling you when you load or unload a bolt to/from the crossbow.
  • Remaining bolt stack sprite properly updates when loading the crossbow with a single one.
  • You now only unload a bolt from the crossbow if you interact with an empty hand on a loaded crossbow you are currently holding.
  • Energy use of powered crossbow was made more efficient, 50 instead of 100.
  • Fire Delay of powered crossbow was lowered, 6 instead of 12.
  • Projectile damage of powered crossbow was buffed, 40 -> 55.
  • Projectile penetration of powered crossbow was buffed 20 -> 30.
  • Bolt projectile sprite was changed to something more fitting
