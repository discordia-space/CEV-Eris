06 August 2022

Humonitarian updated:

  • fixed heatwave light damage range trigger
  • Improved heatwave code
  • various vending machine issues

MLGTASTICa updated:

  • Burst fire can no longer stack
  • Full auto fire_delay is now based on the shown value , no longer on the gun's burst_delay
  • Guns are no longer hard locked to a 4 MS fire_delay.
  • Bull shotgun now has a fire_delay of 2 MS.
  • SEMI_AUTO_NODELAY has been changed to SEMI_AUTO_300, with a firerate delay of 2 MS

SirRichardFrancis updated:

  • text-to-speech
  • ghost not hearing tts
  • mice and other mobs having actual voice

ValoTheValo updated:

  • more holoposters and decorations around maint
  • Shop area in technomancer lobby
  • Remapped Disposals
  • small buffs to Excelsior and Neotheo Voidsuit to compete with basic armor vests

assortedbeads updated:

  • Added 2 new scabbards and 2 equivalent recipes to obtain them
  • added 4 new sounds, two for holster sheathing and unsheathing, two for scabbard sheathing and unsheathing
  • added 4 new on-mob icons and 2 new icons
  • changed the grammar of some holster text to be less guncentric, since scabbards are a subtype of holsters.
  • ported code from Sojourn to allow holstering swords

pexel5 updated:

  • The ability to take glasses that protect from flash for heads of departments/
  • Sunglasses desc.
